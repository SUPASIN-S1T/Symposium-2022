Trade Logistics Symposium 2022

Logistics services has served as the backbone of international trade and has played a critical role in the drive economy forward more seamlessly despite the limitations caused by the COVID-19 pandemic. Moreover, the logistics sector is responding to support cross border e-commerce demand growth under the new normal era.

For international trade in the context of its changes, the Department of International Trade Promotion (DITP), Ministry of Commerce, Thailand has also continuously supported the growth of this sector with its focus on upgrading the value chain of the high potential logistics services. This has been done through organizing the trade logistics symposium annually to empower and equip entrepreneurs with knowledge and in-depth information regarding the international business and trade.

Trade Logistics Symposium 2022 will be convened in a hybrid format under the theme “Empowering E-commerce and Cross Border Logistics”. The time has come to highlight a breakthrough growth in e-commerce and cross border logistics market. This premier symposium brings the world’s foremost business leaders, experts and international organizations are together to share knowledge and perspectives on the direction of e-commerce and cross Border logistics trends that shaping the industry. Participants will be enhanced preparedness and capabilities for renewing their logistics and support services business.

OBJECTIVE

To empower and develop personnel in logistics and related sectors to gain a similar vision and realize the importance of efficient logistics management and enhancing logistics services to match international standards.

To prepare the competence of Thailand’s logistics providers and international trade operators in developing the competitiveness of the industry in Thailand and in the ASEAN region.

To enhance the capabilities of trade logistics providers andcreate added value in the import and export industry.

To promote networking and cooperation in trade logistics and international trade in the ASEAN region.
